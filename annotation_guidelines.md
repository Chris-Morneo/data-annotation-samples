# Annotation Guidelines

The goal of annotation is to label data accurately and consistently to improve AI
modles 

## Rules
- Follow instructions
- Be consistent across all entries
- Do not guess; use the closest accurate label
- Flag unclear or wrong data

## Text Evaluation Criteria
- Accuracy: Is the information correct?
- Clarity: Is the response easy to understand?
- Relevance: Does it directly answer the prompt?
- Tone: Is it appropriate and neutral?

## Label Definitions
- GOOD: Accurate, clear, and relevant
- ACCEPTABLE: Mostly correct but slightly unclear
- POOR: Incorrect, misleading, or irrelevant
